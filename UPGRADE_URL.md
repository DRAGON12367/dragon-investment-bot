# Upgrade Your Streamlit Cloud URL

This guide shows you how to change or upgrade your Streamlit Cloud app URL.

## Current URL
Your current URL is: **https://ai-investment-bot.streamlit.app/**

## Option 1: Change App Name (Changes URL)

To get a different URL like `https://your-custom-name.streamlit.app/`:

### Steps:
1. Go to [Streamlit Cloud Dashboard](https://share.streamlit.io/)
2. Sign in with your GitHub account
3. Find your app: **ai-investment-bot**
4. Click **"⋮" (three dots)** → **"Settings"**
5. Scroll to **"App URL"** section
6. Click **"Edit"** next to the app name
7. Enter your new app name (e.g., `dragon-investment-bot`, `ai-trading-pro`, `smart-investor`)
8. Click **"Save"**
9. Your new URL will be: `https://[new-name].streamlit.app/`

### Important Notes:
- App names must be **unique** across all Streamlit Cloud
- App names can only contain: **lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens**
- Changing the name will change your URL immediately
- Old URL will stop working (redirects may work temporarily)

## Option 2: Custom Domain (Premium Feature)

To use your own domain like `https://investments.yourdomain.com/`:

### Requirements:
- Streamlit Cloud **Team** or **Enterprise** plan (paid)
- A domain you own
- DNS access to configure CNAME records

### Steps:
1. Go to Streamlit Cloud → Your App → Settings
2. Scroll to **"Custom Domain"** section
3. Enter your custom domain
4. Configure DNS:
   - Add a CNAME record pointing to: `share.streamlit.io`
   - Example: `investments.yourdomain.com` → `share.streamlit.io`
5. Wait for DNS propagation (can take 24-48 hours)
6. Streamlit will verify and activate your custom domain
